About Us

IEEE Students Chapter 12951

IEEE SC 12951 at PSG College of Technology was initiated in 1977. It was started to involve the students in research and encourage new ideas in their respective fields. PSG has always been known for setting benchmarks in terms of quality of engineering and is one of the first and foremost institutions to have a Students branch of IEEE. The IEEE Students Chapter of PSG Tech has been actively involved in organizing top-notch technical fests every year, sparking the interest and involvement of students from various parts of the country. It is the largest technical association at PSG College of Technology.

ATOM 2K26

ATOM 2K26 is the flagship technical symposium organized by the IEEE Students Chapter 12951 of PSG College of Technology. This edition promises to be a grand celebration of innovation, creativity, and cutting-edge technology.

ATOM is an event exclusively involving students from PSG College of Technology and PSG Institute of Technology and Applied Research. The symposium brings together aspiring engineers, innovators, and technology enthusiasts to participate in a diverse range of technical and non-technical events.

With the theme “Unleash the Magic Within”, ATOM 2K26 blends technical excellence with an immersive and magical experience, creating an inspiring and memorable journey for every participant.
